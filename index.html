Infinity Stones Game!!


You will be given a random number at the start of your death wish with Thanos.

There are four gems below. By clicking on one gem you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above random number.


The value of each gem is hidden from your mortal eyes until you click on it.

Each time the battle starts anew, their will be a new value for each gem.
